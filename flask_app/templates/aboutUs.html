About us

Millennium Cinema

  • Millennium is a modern and comfortable cinema with a fantastic location on Tirana Boulevard, where the main cultural institutions are located, including the National Theatre, the Academy of Arts, Tirana Castle, etc. The Millennium Hall is equipped with the latest cinema technology, including a screen with the size of European standards, the Digital Dolby Surround Sound system and the 3 Dimensional Xpand movie system. The hall has a capacity of 260 seats. Millennium, in cooperation with the biggest Hollywood studios such as Universal, Warner Bros, Miramax, Fox, Continental, brings the latest films to the Albanian public at the same time as the world premieres. The hall has a capacity of 260 seats.
